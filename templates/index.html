Our Heritage

Three generations of passion on every plate

Founded in 1985 by the Rossi family, Bella Cucina has been the beating heart of Italian dining in New York City. Every recipe is a love letter to our homeland — crafted fresh, every single day.

Our chefs source only the finest ingredients: San Marzano tomatoes, 24-month aged Parmigiano, and hand-milled Tipo 00 flour. Because great food starts long before it reaches the flame.

A warm neighborhood dining room, a lively open kitchen, and a menu that changes with the season — that is the Bella Cucina rhythm.
